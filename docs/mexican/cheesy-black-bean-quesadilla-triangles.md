- :hamburger: 2 MorningStar Farms chipotle black bean burgers

- :butter: 1 Tbsp melted butter

- :bell_pepper: 2 Tbsp red or green bell pepper

- 1 medium bowl
- 1 pizza cutter
- 1 baking sheet
